Bio 285: Introduction to Systems Biology

Welcome to the course website for Bio 285: Introduction to systems biology! Here you'll find links to all of the key resources for this class. The course syllabus, assignments and links to licensed primary research articles can be found on our sakai.wlu.edu site.

Class

  • Systems biology. What is systems biology? What does it mean to take a systems-level approach to biological questions? What does systems biology mean to us?
  • Sequencing technologies. How do we sequence DNA? How do we sequence entire genomes? We'll consider the trajectory of technological innovations in DNA sequences over the last few decades.

Lab

  • Welcome to R. An introduction to R with an emphasis on why it is an awesome data analysis tool.
